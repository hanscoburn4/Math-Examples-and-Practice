Beginner Examples

Beginner 1

Expression: \( (3 + 5) \times 2 \)

Steps:

\( 3 + 5 = 8 \)

\( 8 \times 2 = 16 \)

Beginner 2

Expression: \( 4 \times (2 + 6) \)

Steps:

\( 2 + 6 = 8 \)

\( 4 \times 8 = 32 \)

Intermediate Examples

Intermediate 1

Expression: \( 6 + 4 \times 3 - 2 \)

Steps:

\( 4 \times 3 = 12 \)

\( 6 + 12 = 18 \)

\( 18 - 2 = 16 \)

Intermediate 2

Expression: \( (5 + 3) \times 4 - 6 \)

Steps:

\( 5 + 3 = 8 \)

\( 8 \times 4 = 32 \)

\( 32 - 6 = 26 \)

Advanced Examples

Advanced 1

Expression: \( \left( \dfrac{2 + 4}{3} \right)^2 - \dfrac{1}{2} \)

Steps:

\( 2 + 4 = 6 \)

\( \dfrac{6}{3} = 2 \)

\( 2^2 = 4 \)

\( 4 - \dfrac{1}{2} = \dfrac{8}{2} - \dfrac{1}{2} = \dfrac{7}{2} = 3.5 \)

Advanced 2

Expression: \( \dfrac{3}{4} + \dfrac{5}{6} \times 2 \)

Steps:

\( \dfrac{5}{6} \times 2 = \dfrac{10}{6} = \dfrac{5}{3} \)

\( \dfrac{3}{4} + \dfrac{5}{3} = \dfrac{9}{12} + \dfrac{20}{12} = \dfrac{29}{12} = 2 \dfrac{5}{12} \)
